Places To visit

Anegudde Vinayaka Temple-

Anegudde Vinayaka Temple

Famously known as ‘Sarva Siddhi Pradaayaka’ temple, is devoted to lord Ganesha. According to legends the lord here is the provider of boon and is believed to fulfil all the wishes of pilgrims.

Kundeshwara Temple

Kundeshwara Temple

The town Kundapura was named after the Kundeshwara temple which is dedicated to lord Shiva. A 4 ft high Shiva Linga where in one-third of the Shiva Linga appears on a pedestal and the remaining is buried beneath it. The holy shrine also worships Goddess Parvati and Lord Ganesha.





Hanging Bridge

Hanging Bridge

Located at the top of a small backwater channel the suspension bridge is a go to place to enjoy the scenic beauty and some alone time with your loved ones. The flowing water and backdrop covered in palm trees with set your travel mood right.

Malpe

Malpe

A hotspot of tourists who love nature, dipping in the ocean and some delicious coastal food. The soft sandy beach also has a wide range of water sports to keep you entertained. Shop some souvenirs from the beach side market or enjoy a boat ride to the mesmerising St. Mary’s Island.

Kodi

Kodi

Beach: Also known as Delta beach, it is perfect location for some photoshoot in the salty air. Covered on three sides by water flowing from Suvarana river and Arabian sea the beach gives away a beautiful natures miracle to witness

Udupi Matha

Udupi Matha

Resembling an ashram the premises of the temple is considered sacred with the presence of a beautiful Kalyani. Devoted to Lord Krishna many tourists and localities visit here in great numbers to offer their prayers.
